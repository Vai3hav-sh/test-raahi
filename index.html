Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Raahi Corporate

Our transparent pricing model typically saves companies 20-35% on their travel costs. Unlike other platforms that have hidden fees, surge pricing, or mark-ups, we offer flat rates year-round. Plus, our lowest price guarantee ensures you're always getting the best deal.

Every hotel in our network undergoes a comprehensive 47-point quality and safety audit. We personally visit each property, verify safety certifications, check cleanliness standards, test Wi-Fi speeds, and ensure business amenities are available. We also maintain a continuous feedback loop with travelers and regularly re-audit our properties.

All our invoices are GST compliant with proper HSN codes and tax breakdowns. We automatically calculate 18% GST on accommodation and provide detailed invoices that are accepted by all accounting systems. Our invoices meet all audit requirements and can be directly integrated with your ERP systems.

We provide 24/7 support through multiple channels - phone, email, chat, and WhatsApp. Business plan customers get a dedicated relationship manager who knows your company's preferences and policies. For enterprise clients, we provide on-site support and custom SLA agreements.

Yes! We offer API integration with popular ERP systems like SAP, Oracle, and Tally. We can also integrate with expense management tools like Concur, Expensify, and Zoho Expense. Custom integrations are available for enterprise customers with our technical team's support.

We offer zero cancellation fees for all bookings made through Raahi. You can cancel or modify bookings up to 2 hours before check-in without any charges. For emergency cancellations, our 24/7 support team will assist you with instant refund processing.
